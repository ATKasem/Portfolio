03 · About

An aspiring engineer who loves the build.

I describe myself pretty simply. I'm an aspiring software engineer who just wants to build fun projects. That curiosity has dragged me across the whole stack, from tiny UI details all the way to backend services that need to stay secure, observable, and dependable.

What I keep coming back to is the spot where AI meets developer tooling. Things that take the friction out of hard work, whether that is turning plain english into a test plan or letting an agent safely reach for real data. I care a lot about clean architecture, thoughtful UX, and actually shipping.
